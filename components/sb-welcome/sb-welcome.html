Welcome to Swedbank!

With about seven million private customers and more than 500 000 corporate customers, Swedbank is the leading bank for the many households and businesses. As a major bank, we are a significant part of the financial system and play an important role in the local communities we serve. By addressing environmental and social risks in our business we contribute to more sustainable companies and communities. Our sustainability strategy is based on integrating sustainability in our core processes, in lending and investment decisions, in procurements and in our payment flows.
